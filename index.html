Transform Your Cash Into the Digital Universe

PayNety offers an innovative cash-to-digital payment solution, allowing users to complete utility payments, mobile top-ups, insurance fees, and money transfers. Our kiosks, planned for deployment in 2025, bridge cash transactions with digital ease. We’re currently securing the necessary licenses to ensure a fully compliant and reliable service.

Mission and Goals

Mission Statement: "PayNety's mission is to simplify financial transactions and provide trusted solutions
that allow users to access digital payment services using cash, easily and securely

Provide fast and convenient payment system We aim to deliver an intuitive platform where users can complete transactions quickly and efficiently. By strategically placing Paynety kiosks in high-traffic areas, users will have access to fast money transfers, bill payments, and more without the need for long queues or complex processes.

Increase accessibility to digital payments Our goal is to bridge the gap between traditional cash payments and modern digital solutions. Paynety kiosks are designed to make digital transactions accessible to everyone, including those without access to online banking or digital wallets. This inclusivity ensures that financial services reach underserved communities.

Simplify financial lives for our users By integrating a wide range of services into a single kiosk, we eliminate the need for users to visit multiple locations for different financial needs. From utility bill payments to mobile top-ups and money transfers, everything is available at their fingertips, saving time and reducing complexity.

Guarantee secure and reliable transactions Security is our top priority. We employ advanced KYC/AML verification processes and partner with trusted technology providers to ensure every transaction is safe, compliant, and reliable. This approach builds trust and guarantees a seamless experience for both users and providers

Why These Goals Matter

These goals define our commitment to delivering an exceptional user experience while creating value for our partners. By focusing on speed, accessibility, simplicity, and security, Paynety positions itself as a trusted leader in the digital payment space.

Development Roadmap

Q1 2025: Foundation Phase
• Company Incorporation: Finalize legal setup and obtain necessary licenses under NY legislation.
• Provider Outreach: Initiate communications with key providers to establish partnerships.
• Kiosk Design and Procurement: Finalize kiosk specifications and initiate orders for the first batch.

Q2 2025: Initial Launch
• First Kiosks Deployment: Install the first 10 kiosks in Brooklyn, targeting high-traffic areas.
• Transaction System Integration: Implement money transfer systems from partner providers into Paynety kiosks.
• Marketing Launch: Begin targeted social media campaigns in groups and other local communities.

Q3-Q4 2025: Expansion Phase
• Kiosk Network Growth: Deploy additional kiosks, expanding coverage across NYC.
• Utility Payment Integration: Add services such as utility bill payments and mobile top-ups to kiosks.
• Feedback and Improvement: Collect user feedback to refine the system and address initial challenges.

Q1-Q2 2026: Service Diversification
• Insurance and Fine Payments: Introduce new services, including insurance premiums and government fine payments.
• Broaden Marketing Efforts: Target communities through online marketing and campaigns.
• Operational Efficiency: Optimize kiosk monitoring and cash collection processes for smoother operations.

Q3-Q4 2026 and Beyond: Scaling and Innovation
• Regional Expansion: Explore opportunities to expand the kiosk network beyond New York.
• Advanced Features: Develop and integrate additional features like QR code payments and loyalty programs.
• Provider Network Growth: Partner with additional financial service providers to enhance offerings.
